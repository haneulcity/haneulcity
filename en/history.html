History

A way from 2015 to current, introduce our journey of HaneulCity.

2013, Startline of our Journey

PAPERBOX started map developing since 'THE LAVA' map, which is 'parkour' type. Later, PAPERBOX developed 'SandCity' and 'ArtCity' map even Minecraft : Pocket Edition didn't support Infinite Map that time.

However, developing a map was not easy. Although Pocket Edition supported Infinite Map since 0.9.0, original SandCity and ArtCity maps are not converted correctly.

Because of that, HaneulCity Project was started to follow two city maps.

2015, First alpha-release of this Map

HaneulCity map was started from a place that currently named as 'Hayang Town'. However, when finding NPC village, lost way to back. Finally, Haneul City was bulit where first-found NPC village is, and 5 villages are building currently.

Future voyage, with open-community

From 2019, HaneulCity map released with open-binary and open-community based map. Meet HaneulCity, and help development of HaneulCity.
